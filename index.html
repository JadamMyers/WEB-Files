Westside Gunn

Background:

Birth Name: Alvin Lamar Worthy
DOB: July 27, 1982 (age 39)
From: Buffalo, New York, U.S.
Occupations: Rapper/Songwriter

Career:

Westside Gunn founded the record lable Griselda Records in 2014, and signed his brother Conway the Machine and cousin Benny the Butcher.
In 2016, Gunn released his first studio album with the title, Flygod. Since then, he has collaborated with artist like Busta Rhymes, Anderson Paak, Pete Rock, and Tyler, the Creator.
Westside Gunn has played at the biggest festivals including, Coachella, Firefly, and Governors Ball. Released on April 17, 2020, his third studio album Pray for Paris,
was his first album to gain mainstream attenttion and was rated highly amongst many music publications. He can now be heard on Kanye West's latest album DONDA.
